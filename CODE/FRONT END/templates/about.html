Welcome to Project

This project reviews traditional diagnostic methods for hepatocellular c arcinoma (HCC) and explores the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) techniques, including machine learning (ML) and deep learning (DL), to enhance early diagnosis.

By comparing approaches like decision trees (DT), random forests (RF), logistic regression (LR), and stacking classifiers, the project highlights the potential of AI in analyzing cancer-related databases, histopathology, radiology, biomarkers, and electronic health records (EHRs) to improve diagnostic accuracy and speed.
