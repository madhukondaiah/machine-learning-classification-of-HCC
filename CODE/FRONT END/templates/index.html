Comparative Analysis of Diagnostic Techniques for Accurate Classification of Hepatocellular Carcinoma

THepatocellular carcinoma (HCC) is increasingly prevalent in developing countries, often diagnosed late, which contributes to high mortality rates. While traditional diagnostic methods remain essential, their limitations necessitate advancements. This review explores the integration of artificial intelligence, particularly machine learning and deep learning, to enhance early HCC detection and diagnosis.
